What We Have Achieved

Impact by Initiative

From classrooms to rescue operations, here is the real-world difference each project creates.

Project Bachpanshala — Education

Hundreds of underprivileged children have received stationery, books, mentorship, and access to quality learning sessions. Our volunteers run regular classes and creative workshops, nurturing curiosity and confidence in every child we reach.

Project Udaan — Women Empowerment

Women across multiple states have gained vocational skills, entrepreneurship knowledge, and legal literacy through Udaan. Many graduates have started their own small businesses, becoming financially independent and inspiring others in their communities.

Project Jeev — Animal Welfare

Thousands of stray and injured animals have been fed, rescued, and provided veterinary care through Project Jeev. Our emergency response volunteers are active across multiple cities, ensuring no animal in distress is left without help.

Project Sewa — Food & Clothing

Regular food distribution drives have served thousands of meals to homeless and underprivileged families. Clothing campaigns during winters have ensured dignity and warmth for vulnerable communities when they need it most.

Project Prakriti — Environment

Thousands of saplings have been planted across communities and educational institutions. Waste management awareness programs and eco-education drives have reached schools and neighbourhoods, embedding environmental responsibility in daily life.

Project Vikas — Skill Development

Youth across India have been trained in digital literacy, trade skills, financial management, and communication. Industry mentors have guided Vikas graduates into employment, bridging the gap between education and a stable career.
