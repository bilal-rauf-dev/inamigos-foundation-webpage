Our Initiatives

Six Projects. One Mission.

Each initiative targets a critical gap in society — from educating children to conserving our environment — creating meaningful, measurable change across India.

Project Bachpanshala

Ensuring quality education for underprivileged children, nurturing young minds and building bright futures through accessible learning programs. Bachpanshala provides stationery, books, mentorship, and a safe space where every child's potential can flourish — regardless of economic background.

Our volunteers run regular classes, workshops, and skill-building sessions that go beyond textbooks to develop critical thinking, creativity, and confidence in India's next generation.

Children's Education

Project Udaan

Empowering women through skill development and financial independence, helping them soar towards a brighter, self-reliant future. Udaan provides vocational training, entrepreneurship guidance, legal literacy, and mental health support to women who are breaking barriers every day.

From stitching and handicrafts to digital skills and business development, Udaan equips women with tools to become breadwinners, leaders, and role models in their communities.

Women Empowerment

Project Jeev

Dedicated to animal welfare including rescue, protection, and feeding of stray and injured animals — spreading compassion for all living beings. Jeev operates feeding drives, veterinary care camps, and rescue operations for animals in distress across multiple cities.

Our volunteer network responds to emergency animal rescue calls, organises adoption drives, and advocates for stronger animal protection legislation and community awareness.

Animal Welfare

Project Sewa

Providing essential food and clothing to underprivileged communities — serving humanity with compassion and unwavering dedication. Through regular food distribution drives, clothing campaigns, and hygiene kit distributions, Sewa ensures that basic human dignity is never compromised.

Sewa operates especially during festivals, winters, and natural disasters — when the most vulnerable need reliable support to survive and thrive.

Food & Clothing

Project Prakriti

Championing environmental conservation and sustainability — one plant at a time — to ensure a greener and healthier tomorrow for all. Prakriti leads plantation drives, waste management awareness campaigns, and eco-education initiatives across communities and educational institutions.

We partner with local governments and schools to embed environmental responsibility into daily life, building a culture of sustainability from the ground up.

Environmental Conservation

Project Vikas

Enhancing employability through skill development programs, creating pathways to economic stability and professional growth for youth. Vikas offers training in digital literacy, trade skills, communication, financial management, and career guidance to young people seeking better futures.

Through partnerships with industry mentors and corporate partners, Vikas bridges the gap between education and employment — turning potential into purpose.

Skill Development
