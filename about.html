Our Story

InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla. We are a Section 8 registered non-profit organization licensed by the Central Government of India, headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh.

We hold 80G & 12A certifications ensuring transparency and tax-exempt benefits for all our donors. Our foundation is also CSR-1 registered, aligned with NITI Aayog, and IAF ISO 9001:2015 certified — reflecting our unwavering commitment to quality, accountability, and national development goals.

With a strong network of professionals, volunteers, and corporate partners across 28 states, we work to bring positive change through six focused initiatives that address the most critical social challenges facing our country.

The Beginning

Founding Story

In September 2020, amid the uncertainty of the pandemic, Govind Shukla envisioned an organization that could unite compassionate individuals from all walks of life under one shared purpose — to serve those who need it most.

What began as a small group of dedicated volunteers in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh has blossomed into a national network spanning 28 Indian states. InAmigos — meaning "Friends Together" — captures the essence of our philosophy: that collective, sustained action by friends united for a cause can reshape communities.

Today, InAmigos Foundation is recognized by the Central Government as a Section 8 company, holds multiple certifications demonstrating our transparency and governance standards, and continues to grow its volunteer family every year.

September 23, 2020 — InAmigos Foundation is established in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh by Govind Shukla with a mission to serve India's most vulnerable communities.

Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To unite driven individuals and create lasting, positive change in society by addressing critical social challenges through education, empowerment, environmental conservation, and compassionate service.

Our Vision

A compassionate, inclusive India where every individual — regardless of background — has access to education, economic opportunity, and a life of dignity and purpose.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Compassion

We approach every cause with empathy, placing the needs of vulnerable communities at the center of all our decisions and actions.

Accountability

Every rupee donated is tracked and directed with full transparency. Our certifications and governance standards hold us to the highest standard.

Inclusion

We build communities that welcome all — regardless of caste, religion, gender, or economic status — united by a shared commitment to service.
