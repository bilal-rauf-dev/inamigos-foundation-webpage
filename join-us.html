Volunteer & Internships

Join the InAmigos Family

200+ changemakers across 28 states. One shared mission. There is a place here for anyone who believes that ordinary people can create extraordinary change.

Why Volunteer With InAmigos?

InAmigos Foundation is more than an NGO — it is a community of driven, compassionate individuals who show up every day to make India a little more equitable, a little more kind. When you join us, you are not filling a form — you are joining a movement.

We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds: students, professionals, homemakers, retirees, and anyone in between. Whether you can give two hours a week or want a full six-month internship, we have a meaningful role for you.

Real Impact

Your work directly reaches beneficiaries — children, women, animals, and communities. No bureaucratic delays, no invisible pipelines.

Certificates & LORs

Receive an official experience certificate and letter of recommendation upon completion of your volunteer tenure.

National Network

Connect with 200+ volunteers across 28 states — professionals, students, and leaders united by purpose.

Skill Development

Build leadership, communication, project management, and domain skills through hands-on work that matters.

Flexible Commitment

Work remotely or on-ground, part-time or full-time. We accommodate your schedule and geography.

Sense of Purpose

The most valuable thing you take away — the knowledge that your life touched others in a meaningful way.
