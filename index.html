Who We Are

A Movement Rooted in Compassion

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla. We are a Section 8 registered non-profit organization licensed by the Central Government, based in Chhattisgarh.

We hold 80G & 12A certifications ensuring transparency and tax-exempt benefits for donors. Our foundation is also CSR-1 registered, NITI Aayog aligned, and IAF ISO 9001:2015 certified — reflecting our commitment to quality, accountability, and national development goals.

With a strong network of professionals, volunteers, and corporate partners across 28 states, we work to bring positive change through six focused initiatives.

Our Initiatives

Six Projects. One Mission.

Each initiative addresses a critical gap in society — from education and empowerment to environment and animal welfare.

Project Bachpanshala

Ensuring quality education for underprivileged children, nurturing young minds and building bright futures through accessible learning programs.

Children's Education

Project Udaan

Empowering women through skill development and financial independence, helping them soar towards a brighter, self-reliant future.

Women Empowerment

Project Jeev

Dedicated to animal welfare including rescue, protection, and feeding of stray and injured animals — spreading compassion for all living beings.

Animal Welfare

Project Sewa

Providing essential food and clothing to underprivileged communities — serving humanity with compassion and unwavering dedication.

Food & Clothing

Project Prakriti

Championing environmental conservation and sustainability — one plant at a time — to ensure a greener and healthier tomorrow for all.

Environmental Conservation

Project Vikas

Enhancing employability through skill development programs, creating pathways to economic stability and professional growth for youth.

Skill Development
